### Overview

Notifications keep you and your team informed about the status and performance of your automations.

### Key Features

1. **Alert Types**
   - Success notifications
   - Error alerts
   - Warning messages
   - Status updates

2. **Delivery Methods**
   - Email notifications
   - In-app alerts
   - Webhook integration
   - Mobile push notifications

3. **Configuration Options**
   - Custom triggers
   - Alert priorities
   - Notification rules
   - Team routing
