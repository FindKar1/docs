Welcome to the Tools section, your central hub for building and managing your web automations. Each tool is designed to simplify and streamline your workflow. Below is a quick summary of what you can do with each tool. Click on any section to explore its full features and functionality in detail.

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card
    title="1. Element Selector"
    icon="mouse-pointer"
    href="/bytespacedocs/new-to-bytespace/tools/Element-selector"
  >
    Easily identify and target elements on any webpage to build reliable and dynamic workflows. Whether it's a button, form, or specific text, this tool helps you pinpoint exactly what your web-agent needs to interact with.
  </Card>

  <Card
    title="2. Automation Blocks"
    icon="cubes"
    href="/bytespacedocs/new-to-bytespace/tools/Nodes"
  >
    Create and structure your automation workflows using modular building blocks. Each block represents an action or decision your agent will take, allowing you to design complex processes with ease and flexibility.
  </Card>

  <Card
    title="3. Test Run"
    icon="vial"
    href="/bytespacedocs/new-to-bytespace/tools/Test-run"
  >
    Run a quick simulation of your automation to validate that everything works as expected. Catch errors and adjust your workflow before going live.
  </Card>

  <Card
    title="4. Run"
    icon="play"
    href="/bytespacedocs/new-to-bytespace/tools/Run"
  >
    Execute your automation in a live environment. This is where your web-agent brings your workflow to life, automating tasks seamlessly on your selected platform.
  </Card>

  <Card
    title="5. Logs"
    icon="list-check"
    href="/bytespacedocs/new-to-bytespace/tools/Logs"
  >
    Monitor and debug your automation's performance in real time. The Logs tool provides detailed insights, tracking each action taken by your agent and highlighting any issues that need attention.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Explore each section to harness the full power of the Tools available to you. Ready to build something amazing? Let's get started!
