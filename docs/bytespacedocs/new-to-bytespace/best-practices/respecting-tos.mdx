### Understanding Terms of Service

Terms of Service (TOS) are the rules and guidelines that govern how you can interact with a website. When automating interactions, it's crucial to understand and respect these terms to maintain account safety and ensure ethical automation practices.

### Finding Terms of Service

Most websites place their TOS in predictable locations:

  <Card title="Common Locations" icon="map-location-dot">

    - Settings/Account pages
    - Privacy Policy sections
    - Developer documentation
  </Card>

  </Card>

### Key TOS Considerations

<Steps>
  1. **Rate Limits**
     - Understand daily/hourly limits
     - Respect cooling periods
     - Monitor usage patterns
  
  2. **Data Usage**
     - Check data collection rules
     - Understand storage requirements
     - Review sharing restrictions
  
  3. **Authentication**
     - Follow login requirements
     - Handle credentials properly
     - Respect session guidelines
  
  4. **Content Interaction**
     - Understand posting limits
     - Review content guidelines
     - Follow engagement rules
</Steps>

### Common TOS Restrictions

<Warning>
Most websites explicitly prohibit:
- Scraping without permission
- Automated mass actions
- Fake account creation
- Bypassing security measures
</Warning>

### When in Doubt

<Note>
If you're unsure about whether your automation complies with a website's TOS:
1. Contact the platform's support
2. Review developer documentation
3. Consult the platform's API guidelines
4. Err on the side of caution
</Note>

### Responsible Automation Checklist

✓ Read and understand the platform's TOS  
✓ Implement required rate limits  
✓ Use appropriate authentication methods  
✓ Monitor and log your automation activities  
✓ Regularly review and update your compliance measures  
✓ Have a plan for handling TOS changes  

<Warning>
Remember: Terms of Service can change at any time. Regular review and updates to your automation workflows are essential for maintaining compliance.
</Warning>
